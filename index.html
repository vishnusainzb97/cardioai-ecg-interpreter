Model Training

Trained on Open-Source Clinical Data

Our model is trained and validated on gold-standard, peer-reviewed ECG datasets with continuous feedback from a panel of practicing cardiologists.

PTB-XL Dataset

PhysioNet — Largest public 12-lead ECG dataset

  • 21,799 clinical ECG recordings
  • 10-second 12-lead recordings at 500Hz
  • 71 unique diagnostic statements
  • Annotated by 2+ cardiologists per record
View on PhysioNet

MIT-BIH Arrhythmia

MIT — Gold-standard arrhythmia reference

  • 48 half-hour two-channel recordings
  • 109,000+ annotated heartbeats
  • 15+ arrhythmia classes
  • Expert cardiologist annotations with consensus
View on PhysioNet

Chapman-Shaoxing

Chapman University & Shaoxing People's Hospital

  • 10,646 12-lead ECG recordings
  • 11 rhythm and morphology classes
  • 10-second recordings at 500Hz
  • Multi-label diagnosed by clinical experts
View on PhysioNet
